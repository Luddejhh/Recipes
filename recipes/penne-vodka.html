Penne Vodka

Description

Quick pasta with creamy vodka sauce, crispy pancetta and freshly grated parmesan. The vodka gives the dish a new, exciting taste that you just have to try!

Ingredients

  • 400 g penne pasta
  • 25 g of butter
  • 125 g pancetta, in pieces (or bacon)
  • 4 tbsp vodka
  • 2 dl whipping cream
  • 3 tbsp tomato puree
  • fresh leaf parsley, roughly chopped
  • salt
  • black pepper, freshly ground
  • Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
  1. Cook the pasta in a large saucepan according to package directions.
  2. Heat the butter in a large frying pan over medium heat, add the bacon and fry for 3-5 minutes until golden. Add the vodka and bring to the boil and simmer for a minute before adding the cream, tomato puree and chopped parsley
  3. Simmer gently for 5 minutes or until the sauce has thickened. Season with salt and black pepper
  4. Whisk together the egg yolks with the finely grated cheese and plenty of freshly ground black pepper in a bowl.
  5. When the pasta is cooked, drain the water and add to the sauce and mix. Serve with grated parmesan cheese.
  6. When the water starts to boil and there is a tablespoon left, remove the pan from the heat. Add the egg mixture, stir until it becomes creamy, adding a little water if it becomes dry. Season with salt and pepper.
