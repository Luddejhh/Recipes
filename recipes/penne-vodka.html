Penne Vodka

Description

Cook the pasta in a large saucepan according to package directions. Heat the butter in a large frying pan over medium heat, add the bacon and fry for 3-5 minutes until golden. Add the vodka and bring to the boil and simmer for a minute before adding the cream, tomato puree and chopped parsley. Simmer gently for 5 minutes or until the sauce has thickened

Season with and black pepper. When the pasta is cooked, drain the water and add to the sauce and mix. Serve with grated parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 400 g penne pasta
  • 25 g of butter
  • 125 g pancetta, in pieces (or bacon)
  • 4 tbsp vodka
  • 2 dl whipping cream
  • 3 tbsp tomato puree
  • fresh leaf parsley, roughly chopped
  • salt
  • black pepper, freshly ground
  • Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
