Carbonara

Description

Cook the spaghetti about 3 minutes shorter than the instructions on the package say. Cut the pancetta into small pieces. Fry the pancetta in a wok or a frying pan with high sides without fat until it becomes crispy. Lift up and set aside. Leave the fat in the pan.

Put the heat back on the pan with the frying fat, pour in about 3 dl of the pasta water and let it boil. Drain the spaghetti and add to the pan with the pancetta. Just like when you cook risotto in a broth, the pasta should be cooked in the frying fat. Let it boil for about 2-3 minutes and stir all the time, this causes the starch from the pasta to be secreted and it becomes a creamy sauce.
